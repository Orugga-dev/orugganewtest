The challenge

Building a lending business from scratch, in a complex regulatory environment

The client needed a complete credit origination platform — not just a frontend, but the entire stack: a user-facing loan application experience, a cloud backend that could handle campaign-driven acquisition flows, and real-time integrations with external credit bureaus and identity providers to score applicants instantly.

The platform also needed to support multiple simultaneous campaigns with different loan parameters, rates, and acquisition funnels — configurable without engineering involvement.

What we built

A fully integrated lending stack

Responsive web frontend

A fast, mobile-first loan application interface designed for conversion. Simple UX flow that guided applicants from interest to submission in minutes.

Cloud-based backend with configurable campaign flows

A flexible backend that allowed the client's team to configure loan products, rates, and acquisition campaigns independently — no code changes needed to launch a new product variant.

Real-time credit scoring via multiple provider APIs

Integrations with multiple credit bureaus and identity verification providers, called in real time during the application flow to score and approve applicants without manual review for qualified users.

Campaign management module

A built-in admin module to create, manage, and track user acquisition campaigns with custom parameters — enabling the business to iterate quickly on offers and channels.
