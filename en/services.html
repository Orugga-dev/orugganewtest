How we work

A delivery process built for real constraints.

We keep the process explicit so clients know what happens between the initial conversation and a system running in production.

1. Framing

Clarify the business goal and hard constraints

We start by understanding the product, the users, the operational risk, and the technical dependencies that could break delivery later.

2. Architecture

Choose the stack based on reliability, not fashion

The architecture follows the delivery goal: AI where it creates leverage, mobile where the product needs it, and stable backend/cloud choices for scale and maintainability.

3. Execution

Build with production in mind from day one

We work through real integrations, operational flows, admin tooling, observability, and quality gates instead of leaving the difficult parts for later.

4. Handover and growth

Support the product after launch

We stay close enough to stabilize, iterate, and expand the system once it is live and exposed to real usage.

FAQ

What kinds of projects are the best fit for Orugga?

Projects that need senior engineering execution, complex integrations, mobile product quality, fintech or regulated flows, AI productization, or legacy modernization without stopping the business.

No. We work on new products, delayed initiatives, existing platforms that need modernization, and systems that already have users but need better reliability or faster execution.

We choose technologies based on delivery risk, maintainability, team fit, operational constraints, and what has already proven itself in similar real-world systems.
